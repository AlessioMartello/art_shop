About

I'm Alessio.
I'm an art enthusiast from London.
I've been drawing, sketching and painting all my life.

I started this charcoal art blog to track my progress and hopefully help others along the way. Keeping a weekly blog makes me accountable! I love to attend life drawing classes in London, sketch in public and make art whenever I can.

I don't claim to be an expert and haven't studied art but I love drawing and reading about other people's journey and techniques; I know I've googled how to draw a charcoal eye more than once!

Follow my blog and improve your art with me!

Go to my blog

I always found that original artwork costs too much to buy.

So I decided to start selling my life drawings at a price a I would be happy to pay. An online store allows me to reach a wider audience. Each drawing has been drawn by me at my favourite life drawing classes in London, as I learn to represent the human form as best I can!.
