Faqs

Who is Artlessi for?

The free blog and video content we offer is aimed at developing artists that want to learn from eachother and that don't have formal training as an artist.

What kind of art can I learn about?

Currently we are geared towards charcoal art, we are always expanding and aim to include painting, collage and all things artsy.

How can I improve my art with Artlessi?

Read the blogs, watch the videos but most importantly - practise! Get involved by sharing your experience in the comments sections. Consider blogging for us, to really boost your learning.

How can I get in contact?

Email us! We'd love to hear from you.

Can I commission some artwork?

Yes please do! Drop us an email we will be more than happy to discuss.

Can I blog for Artlessi?

Absolutely! Email us to register your interest in sharing your wisdom with the community.

What payment method do you accept?

I accept Card payment via Stripe, a PCI Service Provider Level 1 certified payment infrastructure

Will you collect my bank details?

No way, all financial information is encrypted and we have no access to it.

Can I return an item?

An undamaged item can be returned within 14 days, for a full refund.

Has my order gone through?

You should have received an order confirmation upon purchase, please check your junk folder. Feel free to email if not.
